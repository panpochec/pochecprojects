Publications included in my PhD thesis

From the very start of my PhD studies I have percieved my thesis as single research building a cohesive image of the premelting topic, studied in mid-chain alcohols. But in the flow of my work the topic branched and melted back together, with formulation of new ideas, new analytical techniques and my own developement as a scientist. The following 5 papers are the milstones of my studies, on which I had pleasure working with many renowned scientist, from Poland and from around the globe.

Nonlinear dielectric effect in the vicinity of solid–liquid phase transition of 1-decanol.

Pocheć M., Małycha K., Orzechowski K.,
Journal of Physical Chemistry C, 2019, 123, IF: 4.189
This is the paper that have started the premelting studies in mid-chain alcohols. Nonlinear dielectric method was used to study n-decanol and it has provided the concept of dual mechanism of premelting in compounds of this group.

  • Read it here

Premelting phenomena in n-alcohols from nonanol to dodecanol

Pocheć M., Niu H., Ren L., Bai S., Orzechowski K.,
Journal of Physical Chemistry C, 2020, 124, IF: 4.126
In this paper, additional compounds were studied to confirm the dual mechanism of pretransitional changes in alcohols. It has been found, that n-decanol is the outlier, being the only compound undergoing rotator state formation.

  • Read it here

Indicators of premelting in 1-decanol and 1-nonanol studied by FTIR spectroscopy

Pocheć M., Orzechowski K., Rutkowski K.,
Surfaces and Interfaces, 2022, 28, IF: 6.137
This paper is the third part of premelting studies and it is focusing on using IR spectroscopy to identify and describe rotator state formation and surface premelting, using established marker bands as well as proposing and testing new.

  • Read it here

Inside Out Approach to Rotator State in Hydrogen-Bonded System
Experimental and Theoretical Cross-Examination in n-Octanol.

Pocheć M., Krupka K. M., Panek J. J., Orzechowski K., Jezierska A.
International Journal of Molecular Sciences, 2022, 23, IF: 6.208
This work combines theoretical and experimental approaches to develop a unifed description of premelting changes from macro- to micro level.

  • Read it here

Intermolecular interactions and spectroscopic signatures
of the hydrogen-bonded system - n-octanol in experimental and theoretical studies.

Pocheć M., Krupka K. M., Panek J. J., Orzechowski K., Jezierska A.
Molecules, 2022, 27, IF: 4.927
This works combines theoretical and experimental approaches, resulting in comprehensive desription of molecular interactions via OH groups and via alkyl chains.
